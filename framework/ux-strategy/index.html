UX Strategy

Contributor(s): Vik Babu & Thomas Chung

A UX strategy builds upon an organization’s business & product strategies through a shared vision for a product or service from the end user’s perspective.

User Needs

Externally derived goals sourced from identified personas, see user stories & personas

Site Objectives

Business, creative, or other internally derived goals for the platform:

  • Convey the value & core differentiator of our brand
  • Mobile form factor for modern users
  • Analytics to understand how customers are using our service
  • Sensitive to the privacy of energy data
  • Landing page that attracts signups
  • Needs to allow for product extensibility (control & demand response)

Functional Specifications

A detailed descriptions of functionality of the platform that meet user needs, see dashboard canvas.

Interaction Design

Development of application flows to facilitate user tasks:

  • Building.ai Splash Page
  • Onboarding
  • Impact
  • Status
  • Settings

Interface Design

Design of interface elements to facilitate user interaction with functionality

Information Design

Designing the presentation of information to facilitate understanding

Visual Design

Graphic treatment of interface elements & overall brand
